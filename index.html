FAQ

Good to know

Are the ingredients safe to use every month?+

Yes — all our blends use food-grade and cosmetic-grade natural ingredients meant for regular, cyclical use. If you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or on medication, check with your doctor first.

Where do you ship?+

We currently ship across Morocco, with expansion underway to other MENA countries. Retail partners include select pharmacies and health stores.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+

Yes, subscriptions are flexible — pause, skip, or cancel a delivery whenever you like, no fees involved.

Will this replace my painkillers completely?+

For many customers, yes — but everyone's pain is different. If your period pain is severe or unusual, please speak with a doctor rather than relying on any single product.
